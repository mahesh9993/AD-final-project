About Us

Welcome to Jeewaka Products, your source for high-quality spices and herbs. We are a family-owned business that has been providing customers with premium spice blends for over 20 years.

Our passion for cooking and love for the diverse flavors of our Sri Lankan heritage inspired us to create Jeewaka Products. We started out small, blending spices in our home kitchen and sharing our creations with friends and family. As word spread about the delicious flavors of our spice blends, we decided to turn our hobby into a business.

At Jeewaka Products, we believe that good food starts with good ingredients. That's why we source only the freshest and highest quality spices from around the world. Our products are carefully crafted in small batches to ensure that each blend is perfectly balanced and full of flavor.

Being a Sri Lankan-based spice company, we have done an extensive exploration of the spice industry and gathered all the necessary information about Ceylon spices. As a Sri Lankan company, we pride ourselves with being able to access top quality Ceylon spices. We are unique in the sense that we have put on offer our products in various grades and standards to match the different levels of prices.
